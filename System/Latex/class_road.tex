\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a road structure on a city map, capable of determining its traffic state based on surrounding buildings. 

The {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_road}{Road}} class manages its placement on a {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_city_map}{City\+Map}}, adjusts its traffic level based on neighboring structures, and transitions between different traffic states (low, medium, high) depending on conditions.
