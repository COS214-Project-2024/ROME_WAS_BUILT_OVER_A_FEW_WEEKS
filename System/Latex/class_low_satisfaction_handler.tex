\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class that manages population and tax adjustments for low satisfaction levels. 

This handler is responsible for reducing the population by removing citizens through death or emigration and adjusting the tax rate based on a lower income affection rate when the satisfaction level is below 40.
