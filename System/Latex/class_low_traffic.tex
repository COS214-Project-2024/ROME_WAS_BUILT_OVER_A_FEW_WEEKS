\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a low traffic state for a road structure. 

The {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_low_traffic}{Low\+Traffic}} class defines the characteristics and behavior of a road when traffic is low, such as indicating low traffic impact.
