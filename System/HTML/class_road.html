Detailed Description

Represents a road structure on a city map, capable of determining its traffic state based on surrounding buildings.

The Road class manages its placement on a CityMap, adjusts its traffic level based on neighboring structures, and transitions between different traffic states (low, medium, high) depending on conditions.
